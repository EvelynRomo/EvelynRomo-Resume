Welcome, my name is Evelyn Romo and I am currently a student at the University of California, Riverside. I am pursing a major in Business Administration with a concentration in Information Systems. I am searching for internship opportunities around Southern California that will help strengthen my skills in the work place.

A little bit of background about me, I am a first-generation University student with a love for numbers and computers! Growing up in Rialto came with its limitations but I did whatever I could with the few resources I had. Early on in 4th grade I was exclusively selected to enter a position of a "Tek Kid" Tek Kids were students who would be trained in using Microsoft Word and Powerpoint. After completing training, the kids would go around to 1st, 2nd and 3rd grade classrooms and teach the younger students how to work with such programs. This early experience sparked my love for computers and teaching! I am a very quick learner when it comes to using new programs. On top of learning quickly, I have also been granted the ability and patience to teach others. With these abilities, I hope to be in a position in which I could teach others, whether it be in a classroom setting for a one on one mentorship. After all, efficient training leads to efficient learning!

As of today, I have little to no experience in my desired work field. I currently work as a Pharmacy Technician for CVS. This position has required me to learn how to use several databases to work with patient information. These databases include large amounts of health conditions and prescriptions along with sensitive personal information such as addresses, phone numbers, insurance information, and any other crucial information. My job requires a lot of organization and confidentiality. This is why my pharmacy has granted me the position of training all of the new hires. I teach new hires how to be efficient with their time, keep patient confidentiality, remain organized to avoid mistakes, and how to handle different databases in order to carry out specific duties. This position has prepared me to handle a variety of problem solving situations in the best possible manner. By remaining calm and remembering that there is always a solution for any problem I help guide my coworkers into figuring out a problem solving strategy to achieve the best possible outcome. These experiences have molded me into a great critical thinker that is able to cooperate with a team in order to reach a desired and ideal outcome. I am open to gaining new skills and contributing to the growth of my future work place!
